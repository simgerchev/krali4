About

what_it_isA browser-based terminal puzzle. Connect to a simulated machine and follow a trail of clues left by users who may or may not exist.
how_it_worksSessions are isolated. Each run generates a fresh environment — different hostnames, file contents, and hidden paths.
who_its_forAnyone curious about what it feels like to find something on a machine that wasn't meant to be found.
limitsHardened sandbox. No real filesystem access, no network egress, no persistent state. You are the only one in here. Probably.
